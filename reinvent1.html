Reinvent 1: Foundation is designed for emerging leaders who want to build their leadership capabilities on a solid biblical foundation. Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior leadership experience?

No prior formal leadership experience is required. This program is designed for emerging leaders and those new to faith-based leadership development.

What denomination is this program for?

We welcome Christians from all denominations. Our focus is on shared faith in Jesus Christ and biblical principles that transcend denominational differences.

Is this program accredited?

While not academically accredited, participants receive a certificate of completion and continuing education credits for professional development.

What if I miss a live session?

All sessions are recorded and available for makeup viewing. We also provide session summaries and additional support for missed content.

Can my organization sponsor multiple employees?

Yes! We offer corporate packages and group discounts for organizations wanting to develop multiple leaders simultaneously.

What's the next step after Reinvent 1?

Graduates can continue with Reinvent 2: Advanced, join our Leadership Lab, or pursue our Certification Pathway for coaching and training others.
